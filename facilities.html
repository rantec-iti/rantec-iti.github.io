--- layout: page title: Facilities --- Rantec ITI boasts state or the art infrastructure both in terms of building and Equipment. The campus has its own hostel, computer lab and workshops for all the three courses. On campus wifi is available and proper drinking water taps are present in different corners of the premises. The campus is under 24/7 CCTV surveillance for effective security. Qualified working staff and professional teachers are also a key feature of the institution. Apart from the above features the institute possesses all the equipment for practical training in the form of Milling machine, laithe machine, grinding machine, crushing, drilling, welding machine along with AC, DC motors.
